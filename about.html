About Us

IT Insights is dedicated to providing reliable information about IT ethics, best practices, and advancements in technology. Our mission is to help individuals and professionals navigate the rapidly evolving world of information technology with confidence and responsibility.

Behind IT Insights is Gabriel Fares, a junior majoring in BS IT, with a strong passion for exploring everything IT-related. My background in programming, especially with Java, combined with my love for learning and curiosity, drives me to research and share insights on the latest IT trends, ethical considerations, and practical applications.

Outside of academics, I enjoy going to the gym and reading, which helps me maintain a balanced perspective. This passion for technology and continuous learning ensures that IT Insights remains a trustworthy source for both seasoned professionals and newcomers in the tech world.
